Cross-Validation for Spatial Interpolation

As a Research Assistant and graduate student at the University of Toronto, I designed and presented an algorithm for automatic selection of spatial interpolation methods at a daily scale for generating historical fire weather maps. I presented this project in Advanced Statistics and then published an article about it in Earth & Space Science.
