About

Mapping Moth Forest Damage using LANDSAT

As a GIS & Data Assistant at the Ontario Government in 2019, I started working on a project to map moth damage to trees across Ontario using LANDSAT satellite imagery. I continued working on this project as part of my PhD work on a research agreement with the Ministry of Natural Resources & Forestry and presented our results at international conferences in Montreal, QC, Victoria, BC, Los Angeles, USA, and online.
