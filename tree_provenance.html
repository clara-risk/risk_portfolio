Tree Provenance Trial Database

As a Project Biologist at the Ontario Government, I compiled a database of tree provenance trial results. A tree provenance trial is a type of experiment where trees from different locations, or "provenances," are planted in the same area and observed over time to see how they grow and adapt to the environment.
